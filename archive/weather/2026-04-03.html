German Weather Service - Regional Weather Advisory Service Munich, issued
on Thursday, April 2nd, 2026, at 2:30 pm

Weather forecast for the German Alps:

Under weak high pressure, cool maritime air dominates the weather.

Overnight into Friday, it will be mostly cloudy with isolated showers, mainly in the east, which will subside by early morning. The snow line will be around 1000 meters. Temperatures will drop to +3 degrees Celsius in the east and down to -3 degrees Celsius in the west.

On Good Friday, it will be mostly cloudy in the east, with sunshine initially towards the Allgäu region. Cumulus clouds will develop throughout the day, but it will mostly remain dry. Highs will reach 11 to 13 degrees Celsius in the valleys, around +1 degree Celsius at 1500 meters, and around -7 degrees Celsius at 3000 meters. Winds will be generally light, averaging around 10 km/h, with moderate to fresh gusts of around 30 km/h from westerly directions.


Further outlook until Sunday, April 5, 2026: Saturday will be sunny and cloudy with some rain in places. Easter Sunday will be sunny and mild.

Wetterbericht für den Deutschen Alpenraum:

Am Karfreitag ist es nach Osten hin oft bewölkt, Richtung Allgäu zeigt sich anfangs die Sonne. Im Tagesverlauf bilden sich vermehrt Quellwolken, es bliebt aber meist trocken. Die Höchstwerte erreichen in den Tälern 11 bis 13 Grad, in 1500 m um +1 und in 3000 m um -7 Grad.
