German Weather Service - Regional Weather Advisory Service Munich, issued
on Sunday, March 8, 2026, at 2:30 p.m.

Weather forecast for the German Alps:

High pressure, combined with mild air, continues to dominate the weather in the German Alps.

The night leading into Monday will start mostly clear, with increasing cloud cover later, but it will remain dry. Temperatures in the valleys will drop to between +5 and -1 degrees Celsius.

Monday will be partly cloudy to sunny. Isolated brief showers are possible in the afternoon. Temperatures will rise to 14 to 16 degrees Celsius in the valleys, 7 degrees Celsius at 1500 meters, and -2 degrees Celsius at 3000 meters. Winds will remain light.


Further outlook until Wednesday, March 11, 2026: Tuesday will be partly sunny and cloudy, mostly dry. Wednesday will bring some showers. It will remain mild.

Wetterbericht für den Deutschen Alpenraum:

Hochdruckeinfluss bestimmt zusammen mit milder Luft weiterhin das Wettergeschehen im Deutschen Alpenraum.

In der Nacht zum Montag ist es anfangs oft klar, später nimmt die Bewölkung etwas zu, es bleibt aber trocken. Die Temperatur geht in den Tälern auf +5 bis -1 Grad zurück.
