Mountain Weather Report

2026-01-12 (Issued: Sunday, 11.01.2026, at 2.30 p.m.)

German Weather Service - Regional Weather Advice Munich, issued
on Sunday, 11.01.2026, at 2.30 p.m.

Weather forecast for the German Alpine region:

Under the influence of intermediate highs, cold continental air has an effect on the weather. On Monday, a warm front will move in from the west.

In the night to Monday it will be partly cloudy, at times clear. From the second half of the night, clouds will increasingly move in from the west. The temperature in the valleys will drop to -9 to -13 degrees.

On Monday, the clouds will continue to increase from the west and then snow will spread to the east, which will quickly turn into freezing rain. In the afternoon, rain will fall in the west, freezing rain must be expected in the east and in some valleys until the evening. The snow line will rise to about 1700 m during the day, with mostly 1 to 5 cm, in places up to 10 cm of snow. In the Berchtesgadener Land, 15 to 20 cm are possible at summit locations. The temperature will rise to 1 to 6 degrees in the valleys, -1 degree in the east at 1500 m and +3 degrees in the west and at 3000 m to -5 degrees. The wind will blow lightly on average around 10 km/h, in gusts fresh around 20 km/h from the south. In the Allgäu, the wind will blow moderate to fresh in the afternoon at 15 to 20 km/h, in gusts of up to 40 km/h from the southwest. The wind will also increase in the course of the day with gusts of up to 80 km/h from the west.


Further outlook until Wednesday, 14.01.2026: On Tuesday sun and clouds, mostly dry. Mild. On Wednesday variable clouds, rain at night. Still mild. Stormy in summit locations.
