Description

One freerider was skiing alone and was reported missing in the evening. He was found dead in an avalanche by mountain rescue the following morning. He was carrying an activated avalanche transceiver and an airbag, which had not deployed. Several details are still unclear (slope at least 35 degrees, exact altitude, entry point, avalanche dimensions, etc.). Source: Kleinwalsertal Police Inspectorate
