Description

A group of ski tourers observed a cornice collapse, resulting in a wet avalanche. Because objects were swept along in the avalanche path and the group could not rule out the possibility that people were involved, they reported the incident and initiated a search for safety. This search ultimately proved unsuccessful. Therefore, no further data on the avalanche was collected. Source + Image: PI Kleinwalsertal
